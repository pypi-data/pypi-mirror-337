YouTube MPV Bookmarklets

These bookmarklets allow you to play YouTube videos (and other supported sites) in MPV, with an option to archive them to archive.org.

Installation:

Drag one or both of these links to your browser's bookmarks bar:

▶️ MPV Play ▶️ MPV Play+Archive

How to use:

  1. Install the bookmarklets by dragging the buttons above to your bookmarks bar
  2. Navigate to any YouTube video (or other supported site)
  3. Click the appropriate bookmarklet:
    • MPV Play: Play the video in MPV without archiving
    • MPV Play+Archive: Play the video in MPV and archive it to archive.org

Supported Sites:

All sites supported by yt-dlp should work, including:

  • YouTube
  • Vimeo
  • Dailymotion
  • And many more!
